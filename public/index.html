Muttichira Bayanul Uloom Dars is located in the historic Muttichira Shuhada Juma Mosque, which is situated near Talappara Town.

Read More About Us

Student

Our Dars has more than 90 Students and under the managment of six Usthad

Education

It is an important wing of BUSS under the control of experienced faculties in different educational sectors.

Sahithya Samajam

Bayanul Uloom Sahithya Samajam is a platform for the literary and non literary activities of students. The activities of the Samajam begin after the official inauguration every year. All extra curricular activities conducted by the Bayanul Uloom Dars are under the auspicious of Sahithya Samajam. It aims the Islamic literary and artistic development of the students

Library

It is a collection of books for explore extra curricular activities.

Magazine

Magazines impulse the writing abilities of a student. AL Bayan Magazine is publishing to improve the writing and literacy skills of students.

Smart Class

It is a virtual platform for expanded research in religious studies.

Computer Lab

It is a space provides Computer Services to students. There are 8 computers for 92 students. Alhamdulillah.

Store

A dedicated store that provides stationery, books, and daily essentials for students, ensuring they have everything needed for their learning journey.

Medical Wing

A dedicated Medical Wing ensures the health and well-being of all students, with first-aid facilities, health check-ups, and care support within the Dars premises.

Eligibility

Students who completed basic Islamic education are welcome to apply for advanced religious studies.

Curriculum

Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, Arabic language, and modern subjects guided by experienced Usthadh.

Facilities

Library, Computer Lab, Smart Class, Medical Wing & Store for holistic student development.
