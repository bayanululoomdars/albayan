About Us

Discover the heritage, legacy, and scholarly framework of Muttichira Bayanul Uloom Dars

Know Us

The Muttichira Shuhada Memorial Bayanul Uloom Dars is a distinguished system of Islamic education rooted in tradition, spirituality, and character development. Inspired by the prophetic model of Ahlus-Suffa, which began at the Prophet's (PBUH) mosque in Madinah, this system has spread across the world as an exemplary educational framework. Since the advent of Islam in Kerala, mosque-based Dars education has played a pivotal role in shaping the religious and social fabric of the community.

The Muttichira Dars was established under the guidance of the late Shaikhuna Muhammad Musliyar of Thrippanachi, a prominent Sufi leader of Malappuram. With the direction of Ustad Ibrahim Baqavi Al Haithami, a distinguished scholar and a member of Samastha Kerala Jamiyyathul Ulama Malappuram district Mushawara, this Dars now stands as a beacon of learning, with six Ustads and over a hundred students.

Operating for over nine years, the Dars has modern facilities, including smart classrooms and a computer lab, blending traditional Islamic teachings with contemporary education to prepare students for spiritual, academic, and social excellence.

Spiritual Highlights

The legacy of Thrippanachi Ustad continues with regular spiritual practices like:

  • Monthly Jalaliya Ratheeb sessions.
  • Daily Fatihah recitations for the pious.
  • Special events like Al-Badriyah al-Khamisiyah and Mampuram Salath.
  • Post-Fajr and post-Jumuah Yaseen recitations at the Shuhada gravesite.

Extracurricular Activities

Dars Fests: Showcasing student talents in literary, cultural, and academic domains, achieving recognition at state and regional levels.

Sahitya Samajam (Literary Society): Weekly debates, discussions, and creative writing sessions to foster intellectual growth.

Publications: Student-produced magazines and supplements under the supervision of Ustads.

Curriculum & Study Methodology

Combining in-depth study of classical Islamic texts with modern subjects, the curriculum incorporates:

  • Continuous evaluation and semester examinations.
  • Comprehensive learning in Arabic, Urdu, English, and Malayalam.
  • Practical training in preaching and community engagement.

Study Methodology: Guided by six experienced Ustads, the students are divided into six batches. Senior students get practical teaching opportunities. There is dedicated supervision for each area of learning, including academics, arts, and discipline.

Sheikhuna Muhammad Musliyar (Thrippanachi Usthad) Founding Spiritual Inspiration

Sheikhuna Muhammad Musliyar, widely known as Thrippanachi Usthad, was a revered Sufi scholar who served as the spiritual leader for the people of Thrippanachi Palakkad in Malappuram district. As a distinguished spiritual guide and Islamic authority, he laid the foundation for what would become the Bayanul Uloom Dars network.

His profound influence on Islamic education in Kerala extended beyond his lifetime through his directives and spiritual guidance. It was under his specific instruction that Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi Al Haithami established the Muttichira Shuhadha Memorial Bayanul Uloom Dars, marking the beginning of a significant educational institution.

Spiritual Legacy & Connection:

Even during Thrippanachi Usthad's lifetime, Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi and his students would visit him monthly for spiritual guidance and to participate in special prayers (Salawat). This spiritual connection continues even after his passing, with regular visits to his resting place maintaining the spiritual bond.

The Bayanul Uloom Dars preserves Thrippanachi Usthad's spiritual legacy through:

  • Monthly Jalaliyya Rateeb conducted with the ijazah (permission) from Thrippanachi Usthad.
  • Daily recitation of Fatiha in the names of great scholars at the beginning of the first dars after Maghrib prayers, as directed by the great Sayyid Hamid Koyamma Thangal.
  • Regular visits to the tombs of the Muttichira Shuhadha (martyrs), with recitation of Yasin and supplications after Subhi and Jumu'ah prayers.

Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi Al Haithami Usthad Current Leadership & Principal Mudarris

Early Life & Family Heritage

Born into the respected Paruvingal family, Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi Al Haithami grew up in an environment rich with Islamic knowledge and scholarship. His father, Muhammad Faizi, served as a prominent Khatib (ഖത്വീബായ മുഹമ്മദ് ഫൈസി) (sermon-giver), providing the early foundation for Sheikhuna's religious education. The family's multi-generational commitment to Islamic scholarship created an ideal environment for nurturing his intellectual and spiritual development.

Educational Journey

Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi pursued higher Islamic education at prestigious institutions:

  • Baqiyathus Salihath, Vellore: Earned his Baqavi degree with specialization in Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence), Hadith (Prophetic traditions), and Tafsir (Quranic interpretation).
  • Darussalam, Nandi: Completed his Haythami degree with first rank, demonstrating exceptional mastery of advanced Islamic jurisprudence and religious sciences.

His education was shaped by notable teachers who helped develop his scholarly approach:

  • Muhammad Baqavi Pookkottur: Guided Sheikhuna's advanced studies in Islamic jurisprudence, Hadith, and theology.
  • Anwar Abdullah Baqavi Falfari (അബ്ദുല്ല ഫൾഫരി): Contributed to refining Sheikhuna's understanding of Islamic law and principles.
Distinguished Career & Teaching

Following his education, Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi served as a Mudarris (teacher) at several Islamic institutions:

  • Anwarul Huda Arabic College, Ramapuram
  • Kolathur (കൊളത്തൂർ) Mosque
  • Tharayittal Mosque
  • Kurumbathur Mosque
  • Mylappuram Juma Masjid
  • Kottakkal Puthur Mosque
Current Leadership Roles
  • Samastha Kerala Jamiyyathul Ulama: Member of the Malappuram District Mushavara (consultative committee).
  • Jam'iyyathul Mudarrisīn: State Committee Member.
  • Jam'iyyathul Mudarrisīn - State Examination Board: Member.
  • MH English School, Edappal: General Secretary.
Recognition

In acknowledgment of his exceptional contributions to Islamic education spanning over many years across multiple disciplines, Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi was honored with the prestigious Pookkoya Thangal Memorial Award for Best Mudarris.

The Parent Institution & Scholarly Lineage

The Muttichira Shuhadha Memorial Bayanul Uloom Dars belongs to a larger network founded by Sheikhuna Usthad Muhammed Baqavi Pookottoor, a prolific scholar who authored over 500 books on Islamic studies in both Arabic and Malayalam.

Many scholars who studied under Sheikhuna Usthad Muhammed Baqavi Pookottoor have established their own Bayanul Uloom Darses, creating a broad scholarly network dedicated to Islamic education. Sheikhuna Ibrahim Baqavi Al Haithami is among these distinguished students who have carried forward this educational legacy.

Core Objectives
  • To nurture scholars well-versed in authentic Islamic texts.
  • To produce leaders capable of addressing modern challenges with Islamic insights.
  • To provide spiritual guidance and mentorship to the community.
Special Features

  • Integration of modern academic education with recognized universities.
  • Focus on Islamic attire, culture, and discipline.
  • Proficiency in Arabic, Urdu, English, and Malayalam languages.
  • Computer literacy and practical training for students.
  • Weekly literary and leadership activities for holistic development.
